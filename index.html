About Me

I'm a software engineer with experience in building full-stack web applications.

The most recent project I worked on is a video sharing platform like YouTube, which integrated Google Auth for users to log in and users can upload their video to an Amazon S3 bucket. The backend I used for this project is Ruby on Rails. Meanwhile, I also worked on an online Pictionary game that multiple players can create and join a room, play Pictionary with other players, and chat with others. I used MongoDB for the backend database, React-Redux for the frontend and Socket.io for chatting and live drawing.

In addition, I studied Optical Engineering during my undergraduate years, then I got a master's degree in Electrical Engineering. When I took the image signal processing course when I was in graduate school, I found myself passionate about programming, so I taught myself python and started looking into the Computer Engineering industry.
